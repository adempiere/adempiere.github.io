# Payment Details (Cash)


Payment Detail Report for Cash

Help
## Help

Type adjusted payments (receipts positive, payments negative) with allocated and available amounts for Cash transaction

Parameters

**Description**

**Help**

### Business Partner

**Description**

**Help**
